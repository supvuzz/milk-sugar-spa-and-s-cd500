Frequently Asked Questions About Facial Treatments

What types of facial treatments do you offer at Milk & Sugar Spa and Salon in Fort Mill?
We offer a comprehensive range of facial treatments to address various skin concerns. Our signature services include HydraFacials for deep cleansing and hydration, anti-aging facials with peptides and retinol, acne-clarifying treatments for problematic skin, and gentle sensitive skin facials using soothing botanicals. We also provide chemical peels ranging from light glycolic peels to deeper TCA treatments, microdermabrasion, and customized European facials. Each treatment is tailored to your specific skin type and concerns after a thorough consultation with our licensed estheticians.
How much do facial treatments cost and do you offer any packages?
Our facial treatments range from $85 for a basic cleansing facial to $200 for advanced treatments like HydraFacials or chemical peels. Express facials (30 minutes) start at $65, while our signature 90-minute luxury facials are $150. We offer several money-saving packages including a 'Monthly Glow' membership at $120/month that includes one facial plus 20% off additional services, and seasonal packages like our 'Summer Skin Prep' series of three treatments for $350 (regularly $405). We also provide first-time client discounts of 15% and accept HSA/FSA cards for certain medical-grade treatments.
Are your estheticians licensed and what should I expect during my first facial appointment?
All of our estheticians are fully licensed by the South Carolina Board of Cosmetology and have completed additional advanced training in medical-grade skincare treatments. During your first visit, we'll begin with a 15-minute skin consultation where we analyze your skin using a magnifying lamp and discuss your concerns, lifestyle, and current skincare routine. The facial itself typically takes 60-75 minutes and includes double cleansing, gentle exfoliation, extractions if needed, a customized mask, and moisturizer with SPF. We'll also recommend a personalized at-home skincare routine and discuss treatment frequency based on your skin's needs.
How often should I get facials and when will I see results?
For optimal skin health, we recommend facials every 4-6 weeks to align with your skin's natural renewal cycle. However, the frequency depends on your specific concerns - those with acne may benefit from treatments every 2-3 weeks initially, while maintenance clients might come every 6-8 weeks. You'll typically notice immediate improvements in skin texture and hydration after your first facial, with more significant changes in skin tone and clarity appearing after 3-4 consistent treatments. For anti-aging concerns or acne treatment, we usually recommend a series of 6 treatments over 4-5 months for best results, followed by monthly maintenance sessions.
Do you guarantee results and what's your policy if I'm not satisfied with my facial?
While we can't guarantee specific results due to individual skin variations, we stand behind the quality of our services and use only professional-grade products proven effective for various skin types. If you're not completely satisfied with your facial experience, please let us know within 48 hours and we'll work with you to address your concerns - this may include adjusting your skincare routine, scheduling a complimentary follow-up consultation, or applying your payment toward a different treatment better suited to your needs. We also provide detailed aftercare instructions and are available for questions during your skin's adjustment period, especially after chemical peels or intensive treatments.
